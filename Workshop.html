Washington Area Economic History and Development Workshop


The second one-day Washington Area Economic History workshop was held on 14 January 2014 in Arlington Virginia (George Mason University).


The first was held in July 2013.   This is an informal one-day workshop for economic historians and development economists in the greater DC area.  The next workshop will be held in the summer of 2014. It is open to graduate students and faculty visiting from elsewhere.  Those interested in attending or presenting should contact Mark Koyama at Mark.Koyama (at) Gmail.com.
